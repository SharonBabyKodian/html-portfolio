The Best Movies According to Sharon

My top 3 movies of all time

The Good Dinosaur

This movie is about a dinosaur named Arlo who gets separated from his family and has to find his way back home with the help of a human boy. It's a heartwarming story about friendship and bravery.

Harry Potter

It is a magical movie bansed on a story written by J. K. Rowling

Frozen

This movie is about two sisters, Elsa and Anna, who live in the kingdom of Arendelle. Elsa has magical ice powers that she struggles to control, leading to an eternal winter in their kingdom. The story follows their journey to reunite and save their home, highlighting themes of love, family, and self-acceptance.
